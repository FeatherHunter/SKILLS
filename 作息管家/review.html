BUG-1 print_records 解包 dict 为 tuple schedule_cli.py:176
get_records_by_date() 返回 dict 列表，但 print_records 和 print_detail 用 tuple 解包语法：
_, ts, te, dur, act, cat, src_cnt, src_ts, reasoning = rec
# 应改为：
ts, te, dur, act, cat = rec['time_start'], rec['time_end'], rec['duration_minutes'], rec['activity'], rec['category']
src_cnt, src_ts, reasoning = rec['source_contents'], rec['source_timestamps'], rec['analysis_reasoning']
